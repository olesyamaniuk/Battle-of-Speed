Game description

Battle of Speed delivers the ultimate high-speed racing experience, combining adrenaline-pumping action with intense police chases. Take control of a powerful 250cc motorbike and push your limits as you race through challenging, vibrant tracks.

The real challenge? Avoid getting caught by relentless police forces determined to bring you down!Compete in multiplayer showdowns, execute daring maneuvers, and prove you're the fastest rider on the streets.
Do you have what it takes to outrun the law and dominate the race?
